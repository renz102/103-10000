The inspiration for this website stems from a combination of two key influences: the onion domain structure used by the Tor network and a hair shop I encountered in 2015 through a random Discord server. First thing ive seen was that they deceived people in the homepage by saying "we sell flowers" just to be suprised by images of people, with their personal information, and their exact location in the word. The onion domain, known for its layered anonymity and secure routing, served as a metaphor for the website's approach to privacy, structure, and accessibility. Additionally, the idea of creating an online space for a hair shop was sparked by a chance encounter with a small business in 2015, which left a lasting impression. The website combines these elements to offer a unique, user-friendly platform that blends security (I couldnt say for sure since they used crypto mainnets for payments), ease of navigation, and a focus on hair services.
